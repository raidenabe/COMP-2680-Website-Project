FAQ

  1. Are your ingredients fresh?

    Yes, all of our ingredients are sourced fresh.

  2. What days are you open?

    We are open everyday! To find our hours of operation, visit the 'Contact Us' page.

  3. Do you do delivery or is it carryout only?

    We provide both carryout and delivery options.

  4. Do you have vegetarian options?

    Currently, we have one vegetarian option, but we plan on expanding our menu in the future.

  5. How can we contact you?

    For further details, visit our contact page.

Pizza Time is here in Kamloops to supply all your pizza needs! Eat in or out at our brand new location in Kamloops. We offer a variety of different pizzas including vegetarian options. For any questions you can contact us or look at our handy FAQ!

We have many different options to choose from! Our menu includes classics such as Pepperoni, Cheese, and Hawaiian, as well as our signature offerings including BBQ Chicken, Margherita, and Vegetarian.

All of our pizza is prepared by hand with the highest quality ingredients. It is then cooked in our traditional wood-fired pizza oven alongside oak wood to create a rich, smoky flavour.
